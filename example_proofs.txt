# Example proofs for testing the Logic Bot visualizer

PROOF: Modus Ponens
PREMISE: p --> q
PREMISE: p
CONCLUSION: q

PROOF: Hypothetical Syllogism
PREMISE: p --> q
PREMISE: q --> r
CONCLUSION: p --> r

PROOF: Proof by Contradiction
PREMISE: !p --> q
PREMISE: !p --> !q
CONCLUSION: p

PROOF: Disjunctive Syllogism
PREMISE: p | q
PREMISE: !p
CONCLUSION: q

PROOF: Complex Example
PREMISE: (p & q) --> r
PREMISE: s --> (!p | !q)
PREMISE: !s
CONCLUSION: r

PROOF: De Morgan's Law
PREMISE: !(p & q) --> r
CONCLUSION: (!p | !q) --> r
